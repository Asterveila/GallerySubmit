To submit an icon to the Gallery, please first submit your icon files on the "Icon Files" panel, and fill up all necessary fields in the Icon Info and Icon Colors panels!

Once you're happy with your icon info, colors, and made sure the preview looks good, click the "Download .gdicon" button to download your icon file for submission!

Then, click on "Submit to Gallery" to head to the portal page.
On the portal page, simply upload your .gdicon file and send it over for review!
